About Me

My name is Shaw Eastwood. I am a BSc Computer Science graduate from Robert Gordon University with an interest in all things technical and a free-software enthusiast.
Over the past four years I have been slowly migrating over to Linux in all aspects of my computing workload and have, in the past six months, found a great appreciation in the Unix and, especially, the Suckless philosophies. This has led me to my current love of the CLI (command-line) which allows, ideally with vimkeys.
I am currently employed as a Software Engineer, working on projects with a PHP and C# stack. My goal is learning C to pursue a career working on embedded devices and control systems. I am fortunate to be allowed to utilize my preferred technology stack when working with these technologies, most importantly Vim, but also dwm, which I have found to be almost essential to my workflow.

Through my work on front end portals and client facing interfaces, I have developed an appreciating for (good) UX, a facet of development I believe is sorely overlooked.
