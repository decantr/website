about me

My name is Shaw Eastwood. I am a BSc Computer Science graduate from Robert Gordon University with an interest all things technical and a free-software enthusiast.
Over the past four years I have been slowly migrating over the Linux in all aspects of my computing worklaod and have, in the past six months, found a great appreciation in the Unix and especially the Suckless philosophies. This has led me to my current love of the CLI (command-line) and ncurses interfaces, ideally with vimkeys.
I am currently working towards learning C to persue a career in Software Engineering, working on embedded devices and control systems.
